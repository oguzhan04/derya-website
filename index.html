Product surface

Built around the way a forwarding desk actually works

The CRM code is dense because the product is dense. That is a feature. Freight teams need context, state, and action in the same place.

Workbench

Focused job command center

Status pipeline, shipment timeline, email threads, quote PDF, documents, and carrier rates stay attached to the load.

Inbox

RFQs become structured work

Inbound requests are classified, linked to jobs, parsed into origin, destination, mode, cargo, target price, and customer context.

Jobs

Tables for operators, not tourists

Reference, customer, route, equipment, target price, margin, notes, and custom columns are visible without forcing a separate report.

Agent

AI that knows the desk

Customers

CRM with shipment context

Control Center
