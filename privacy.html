Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Derya Maritime Inc ("Derya", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, request a quote, or book a shipment (together, the "Services"). We are committed to handling your information responsibly and only for the purposes described below.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide, such as your work email, company name, and phone number.
  • Shipment details you submit, such as origin and destination, cargo type, weight, dimensions, customs preferences, and any attachments (for example, a safety data sheet).
  • Technical data automatically collected when you visit the site, such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed, used to keep the site secure and to improve it.

2. How we use your information

  • To prepare quotes and respond to your inquiries.
  • To arrange, book, and manage your shipments, including customs coordination.
  • To communicate with you about your shipments and our Services.
  • To meet legal, tax, and regulatory obligations.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of our Services.

3. Sharing your information

We share information only as needed to deliver the Services — for example, with carriers, customs brokers, handling agents, and service providers who help us operate (such as hosting and form-processing providers). We may also disclose information where required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

4. AI-assisted processing

We use automated and AI-based tools to help generate quotes and recommendations. The shipment details you submit may be processed by these tools, and outputs are reviewed by our team before they are acted upon.

5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal and accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To make a request, contact us at info@usederya.com.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard the data you entrust to us.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@usederya.com.
