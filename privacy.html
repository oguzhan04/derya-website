Derya Brain

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Derya Brain is an iOS app for Derya operators to capture RFQs, review load context, add notes, and ask job-context questions. This policy explains how the current app handles data.

Data stored by the app

The current Derya Brain app stores RFQs, notes, selected loads, and assistant messages locally on your device. This local data is used to operate the app experience on that device.

Data collection

The current Derya Brain app does not require an account, does not use advertising networks, does not use third-party analytics, and does not transmit app activity to Derya servers.

Support requests

If you contact Derya for support, we may use the information you provide, such as your email address, screenshots, and issue details, to respond to your request and improve the app.

Future changes

If Derya Brain later adds account sync, hosted APIs, analytics, or other server-side features, this policy will be updated before those features are distributed.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@usederya.com.
